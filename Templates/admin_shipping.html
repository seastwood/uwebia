Shipping & delivery rates

Define one or more rates customers can pick at checkout. The buyer sees them as a radio list on the Stripe-hosted payment page. Use "Shipping" for physical-goods rates, "Local delivery" for in-area courier rates, or "Both" for a single flat rate that works either way.

Naming tip: Local-delivery rates are auto-prefixed with "Delivery — " on Stripe's page so buyers don't see them filed under "Shipping". Stripe's own section header is fixed and reads "Shipping" regardless — that's their UI, not yours.

Local delivery area

Only applies to products with fulfillment type Local delivery. Paste the postal / ZIP codes you deliver to (one per line or comma-separated). Buyers outside these codes will see an "outside our delivery area" message at checkout.

Leave blank if you'll deliver anywhere (e.g. you ship and manually reject out-of-range orders).

{# ─ Tip settings ────────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

Customer tips

Adds a tip selector to the checkout page. Customers can pick a preset or enter a custom amount. Tips are sent to Stripe as a separate line item and are not taxed.

Comma-separated whole-number percentages. The buyer always has a "Custom" option in addition to these.

{# ─ Customer support toggle ─────────────────────────────────────── #}

Customer support chat

When enabled, buyers see a "Need help?" live chat on their order pages. You'll get an unread badge in the navbar and an email when a buyer sends a message.

{# ─ Pickup scheduling ──────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

Pickup hours

When the buyer's cart contains a pickup product, they'll see a day & time picker on checkout limited to these hours. Toggle a day off to mark it closed.
